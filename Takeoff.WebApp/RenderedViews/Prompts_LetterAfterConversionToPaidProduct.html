Welcome to the New Takeoff

In case you haven't heard, Takeoff has come out of its Beta period and is now a paid product. We've converted your account to the least expensive plan, $9.99/month, which can be changed at any time. And...

You get 20% off for life!

Usage until now won't count, so don't worry about all the videos or productions you already have in the system. You have 30 days to decide if you want to be a Takeoff customer. With a paid product, we can invest more resources into making Takeoff better. Here's our plans:

  • Ability to password-protect your productions so clients don't need to create an account.
  • We are working hard on an iPhone app for Takeoff. Expect it in the app store within a few months.
  • Visual customizations. You can replace the Takeoff logo with your own. Eventually you'll be able to change much more.
  • Other nifty features: data export, login with Facebook or Google, asset folders, better and faster upload, and simple ways to move videos throughout productions.
  • For you video producers, we are building a Mac desktop app. It'll sport rock solid uploads, a slick interface, and possibly drop folders. Most importantly, it'll provide integrations with editing programs, starting with Final Cut Pro.

Keep an eye on our blog, because we'll be sharing our progress as we go. It is our goal to build the easiest, best video collaboration system on the planet. It can only happen with your support. I hope you'll join us. Also, I'd love to hear from you personally, so feel free to write me at any time.
