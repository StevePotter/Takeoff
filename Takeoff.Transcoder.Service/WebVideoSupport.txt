this document describes the known parameters for videos that should play fine within Takeoff without requiring transcoding

General support:
'Format' = 'Flash Video'		flv
'Format' = 'MPEG-4'		movs, mp4s, f4v

Video supported:

'Format' = 'VP6'  used by flvs a lot
'Format' = 'AVC' used by mp4s and movs (h264)

Audios supported
audio 'Format' = 'MPEG Audio'
audio 'Format' = 'AAC'
